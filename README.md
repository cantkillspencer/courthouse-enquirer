# The Courthouse Enquirer 🗞️

**CMM 620 — Conflict & Communication | University of Hartford | Spring 2026**

Team 2: Spencer Grimes • Abby Boya • Hazel Tuning

---

An interactive tabloid-style conflict communication analysis of the **Johnny Depp vs. Amber Heard** defamation trial (2022).

### Sections
- **Front Page** — The trial that broke the internet
- **The Cast** — Courtroom sketch portraits & character profiles
- **Scandal Files** — The Bed Incident, Little Richard, The Recordings, The Pharmacopeia, Australia
- **The 5-Stage Conflict Cycle** — Cahn & Abigail's framework applied
- **Theory Files** — Social Exchange Theory & Systems Theory
- **The Verdict** — What the jury said. What conflict theory says.

### Framework
- Cahn, D. D., & Abigail, R. A. (2014). *Managing Conflict Through Communication* (5th ed.). Pearson.
